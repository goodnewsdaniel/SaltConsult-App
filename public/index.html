About Us

Salt Consult Ltd, a Scotland-based consulting firm, was founded in 2019 with the sole aim of improving the economic power of the Black ethnic group in Scotland. Having identified a systematic gap between the Black community and the Scottish society, Salt Consult hopes and works towards closing that gap by collaborating with the Scottish government and various business organizations.

To achieve her aim, Salt Consult tackles three major areas, and they are Diversity, Inclusion, and Training.

Diversity

Diversity is any dimension that can be used to differentiate groups and people from one another. In a nutshell, it’s about empowering people by respecting and appreciating what makes them different, in terms of age, gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, sexual orientation, education, and national origin.

Inclusion

Inclusion is an organizational effort and practices in which different groups or individuals having different backgrounds are culturally and socially accepted and welcomed, and equally treated. These differences could be self-evident, such as national origin, age, race and ethnicity, religion/belief, gender, marital status and socioeconomic status or they could be more inherent, such as educational background, training, sector experience, organizational tenure, even personality, such as introverts and extroverts.

Training

Training and education are a key part of Salt Consult and we aim to get every black person in Scotland in the right fields of work and society thereby lifting the black community out of deprived areas in Scotland. Training will include every aspect of the society from sports, politics, engineering, armed forces, entrepreneurship.

Programmes Under Diversity

At Salt Consult, we recognize that the black community in Scotland is very diverse in terms of age, gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, sexual orientation, and national origin.Africa being the largest continent with the Black population and where most of the black population in Scotland come from, we recognize the cultural difference, and we aim to bring all these together.

Also, with other Black people coming from other parts of the world and 1st and 2nd generation Black Scottish who were born here in Scotland, we will want to bring all these differences together to give the Black community a sense of Economic Importance.Thus, programmes under Diversity are as follows:

  • Organize community programs for different groups
  • Bridging cultural differences in the Black Community
  • Organize different events showcasing the diversity in the Black community

Programmes Under Inclusion

Salt Consult's Inclusion program is aimed at giving the Black community in Scotland a sense of belonging. Salt Consult will work with the Black community to create an Inclusive culture that will make the Black community in Scotland to feel respected and valued for who they are as an individual or/and a group. Inclusion often means a shift in an organization’s mind-set, systematic discrimination and a culture that has visible effects on the Black community, and that is what Salt Consult will try to do.

Programmes under Inclusion includes the following:

  • Support Small Black Businesses in Start-Up and Set-up
  • Recruit Black people in professional fields
  • Identify gaps in organizational structures in terms of black minority employment
  • Identify gaps in government agencies in terms of black minority employment
  • Identify gaps in the Scottish government in terms of black minority representation
  • Create a platform where all black businesses will be showcased and made visible for people to get information
